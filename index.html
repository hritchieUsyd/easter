🏕️ The Esky Lock

Maggie secured the esky with a four‑digit code

  • All digits are different
  • Divisible by 9
  • First two digits form a two-digit prime less than 40
  • Last two digits form a square
  • First two digits are less than the second two digits
  • The number is greater than 2000
  • The number is not a mirror

🧊 Who tied up the boat?

When we finally found a spot to moor, everyone scattered. Someone went straight in for a swim. Someone else grabbed a drink from the esky and started working on their tan. Another started getting lunch ready. In the middle of all that, the boat did get tied up at least for a while. At some point during the night, the boat quietly drifted from shore. Only Maggie remembers who tied up the boat.

  • Malithi and the person lying on the sunlounger were in the same location, but they were wearing different attire.
  • Grace and the person tying up the boat were in different Locations, yet they were the only two wearing casual clothes.
  • Ciara and the person swimming were both off the boat, but only one of them was wearing togs.
  • Quinn and the person cooking lunch were never in the same Location, but they shared the same attire.

Only Maggie saw who tied up the boat.

Tip: Categorise by Location and Attire.
